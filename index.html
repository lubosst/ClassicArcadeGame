Effective JavaScript: Frogger

The objective of the game is to cross the road, to reach the water without coliding with the bugs.

To achieve that, use the arrow keys to move the player (up, down, left, right).

For every succesfull crossing, you get one point. For every collision with the bug, you loose one point.

Game will reset after every succesfull crossing or after collision. Points will reset only after reloading the page.
